Pinup Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

Plabit (hereinafter "the Developer") provides Pinup (hereinafter "the App") and values the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines how the App handles user data.

1. Personal Information Collected

This App does not collect any personal information.

All memo data recorded within the App (content, priority, notification times, completion history, etc.) is stored solely on the user's device and is never transmitted to any external server.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored only on the user's device local storage. The Developer has no access to the user's data.

  • Memo data (content, priority, notification times, repeat days, etc.)
  • Memo completion history
  • App settings (language, lock screen display, floating button, snooze options, etc.)

Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all data from the device.

3. App Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions. These permissions are used solely to provide app functionality and are unrelated to the collection of personal information.

  • Notifications: Used to remind you of memos at scheduled times.
  • Exact Alarm: Used to deliver notifications at precise times.
  • Display Over Other Apps: Used to provide the floating button feature.
  • Battery Optimization Exemption: Used to ensure notifications work reliably in the background.
  • Foreground Service: Used to display memos on the lock screen and maintain the floating button.

4. Network Usage

This App does not use an internet connection. It does not request network permissions and does not transmit any data externally.

5. Third-Party Sharing

This App does not share any data with third parties. It does not include any external services such as advertising SDKs or analytics tools.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is available to users of all ages, including children. Since no personal information is collected, no additional measures for children's privacy protection are required.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this policy is updated, changes will be communicated through app updates or this page.
